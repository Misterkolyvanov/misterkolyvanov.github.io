Payroll Leave Tracking Solution

Created a complex system for a Portland school district involving 60 separate Google Sheets, use of Google App Scripts, and complex formulas involving programming type logic and SQL (=query) language to run front and back end aspects of application.

Application was Payroll Leave Report System that helped all of the school district administrators (principals, directors, superintendents, etc.) keep track of their vacation and sick leave and to easily report when they had used any of their leave. All leave reported was then amalgamated into reports that went to the Payroll department as well as the Superintendent's office.

I created this system from scratch and used Google Sheets, Google App Script, queries (SQL), Vlookups, date sensitive conditional formatting, and JavaScript/Html.

I documented requirements and pain points by interviewing each user type and then built flowcharts to represent the optimal system. I also created a short YouTube video that explained how to input the leave properly.

Student Safety System

Developed a Student Safety system using Google API's, Javascript/HTML, SQL, Google Sheets, Forms, and Google App Script.

This system helped teachers and administrators within each school know where their students were in real time when they were sent to the library or other locations in the school.

Interviewed school teachers and staff, wrote out user stories, designed look and feel of system to work intuitively with different user personas, i.e. students, staff, and teachers/administrators.

I coordinated with the I.T. department to install check in/out kiosks in several places in each school and as students checked in/out of these locations, staff members could view this data from a centralized Google Sheet. I created a YouTube instructional video for training of staff and administrators.
